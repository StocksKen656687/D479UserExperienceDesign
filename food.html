Restaurants

Taniti currently has 10 restaurants: five serve mostly local fish and rice, three serve American-style meals, and two serve Pan-Asian cuisine.

Groceries

Taniti has two supermarkets and two smaller grocery stores.

Convenience Store

Taniti has one convenience store that is open 24 hours a day.
